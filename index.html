🔹 What are the most common ADHD symptoms in adults vs. children? +

In children, common symptoms include hyperactivity (constant fidgeting, running/climbing inappropriately), excessive talking, difficulty waiting turns, and forgetfulness. For adults, symptoms shift: internal restlessness, chronic procrastination, difficulty completing tasks, emotional volatility, poor time management, and trouble with organization. Proper ADHD assessment Cleveland identifies symptom patterns across the lifespan.

The 24-hour rule suggests that consequences or reinforcements for a specific behavior should occur within 24 hours to be effective — especially for children with ADHD. Immediate or same-day feedback helps reinforce learning and reduces power struggles.
